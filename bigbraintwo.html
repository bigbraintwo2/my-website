Ducks Defying Nature

In ponds where ripples gently play,
Ducks waddle, defying nature's sway.
Feathers resist the wind's strong gust,
Defiance in quacks, a daring thrust.
Against the current, they proudly glide,
Nature's rules, by ducks, set aside.
